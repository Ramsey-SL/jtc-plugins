# /brand-setup

Run the Brand Intelligence Center setup wizard to build your brand profile from scratch.

## What This Does

Walks you through 8 areas of brand and business intelligence using a guided, choice-based wizard. Scrapes your website first to pre-fill as much as possible — you confirm, edit, and fill gaps.

**Output:** A `brand-intelligence-center/` folder with structured files and an assembled `system-prompt.md` that all other marketing skills use as shared context.

## When to Use

- First time setting up a new brand or client
- Onboarding a new project before using any other marketing skills
- Starting fresh after a rebrand

## How to Invoke

Just type `/brand-setup` and provide your website URL when asked.

## Areas Covered

1. Business Foundation — who you are, format, location, one-liner
2. Your Customer — profile, occasions, JTBD, switching dynamics
3. Differentiation — competitors, objections, counter-positioning
4. Brand Voice & Identity — tone, personality, NEVER/ALWAYS, visual identity
5. Proof Points & Goals — ratings, testimonials, conversion goal, focus
6. Digital Ecosystem — social, reservations, delivery, tools, platforms
7. Financial Context — revenue range, streams, goals (optional — say "skip" to bypass)

## Tips

- Have your website URL ready
- You can skip any area by saying "skip" — come back later with `/brand-update`
- The more detail you provide, the better every other skill will perform
- Takes 15–30 minutes for a complete setup
